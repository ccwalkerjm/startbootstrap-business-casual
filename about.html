Meet Our Chef and Owner

Neville Walker Chef

Neville Walker is a product of a 2 entrepreneurs. His Mother, the dressmaker and his Father, the owner of a chain of retail clothing store. At 18 years old, Neville started his own business, a local bar in a busy commercial district in Kingston, Jamaica. This first startup gave Neville the discipline and understanding of managing a business while facing the challenges of human resources and marketing. With fashion in his DNA, Neville took the imitative to launch his own fashion design company in 1991, specializing in custom designs and manufacturing for professionals while gaining the contracts of many major companies for the manufacturing of office uniforms for both male and female. In less than 2 years Neville became one of the most sought after fashion designer in Jamaica.

After migrating to the United States in 2002, it was his passion for cooking that fuelled his drive. Neville began working in the food and beverage industry in 2003 in Caribbean restaurants in New York, Florida and Atlanta, where he spent over 9 years as head chef of Golden Krust. For over 10 years, Neville has performed various responsibilities in the restaurant including, line cook, pastry chef, sous chef and most important, being team leader for over 10 kitchen workers. It's these diverse experiences that has now propel Neville to execute his own vision of a Caribbean restaurant - Island Vibes.

Neville has all the necessary ingredients in making Island Vibes a success, he is extremely personable, fun loving as a host and yet very disciplined and strong in management. Neville is married to Terri and lives in the suburbs of Rockdale County.
